week one

How the Internet works

The internet is a global network of interconnected computers that communicate through a standardized set of protocols.

Here’s a simplified overview of how the internet works:

Basic Components
  • Clients and Servers: The internet consists of clients (devices like computers, smartphones) and servers (machines that store and serve information). Clients request data, and servers provide it.
  • Routers and Switches: These devices direct data traffic across the internet. Routers connect different networks, while switches connect devices within the same network.
  • Internet Service Providers (ISPs): ISPs provide access to the internet. They connect clients to the broader network.
